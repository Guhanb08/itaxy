Privacy Policy

iTaxy acknowledges and respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. Generally, you can browse our website without providing any personal information. This policy outlines how we collect and use your data when needed for services you request or when you choose to share information with us.

Information Collection

Personal information refers to details that may identify an individual, including but not limited to name, email address, phone number, postal address, gender, occupation, and preferences. We do not collect personal data unless you submit it directly. We may log IP addresses for statistical purposes, but this is not linked to your identity unless you are logged in.

We will always inform you about the reason for collecting personal data. Providing this information is optional, but necessary for certain services. You also have the right to access and update your personal information.

Marketing Consent

We provide options to opt out of marketing communications. Unless you opt out, we consider you have consented to our privacy terms. In some cases, explicit opt-in will be required before using your data for specific purposes.

How We Use the Information

Information collected by iTaxy may be used to process bookings or account actions, provide updates on services or promotions, and improve customer experience through better support and personalization.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Your information will not be shared with third parties without your permission, unless required by law or to fulfill a service you've requested. We may share necessary information with service providers working on behalf of iTaxy, who are restricted from using it for other purposes.

Third-Party Websites

The iTaxy website may contain links to third-party sites. We do not share your data with these websites and are not responsible for their privacy practices.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated by updating this section.
